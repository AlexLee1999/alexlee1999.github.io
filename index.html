About Me

Chun-Yen Lee is currently a research assistant in Strategic Networking and Communications Laboratory, Research Center for Information Technology Innovation, Academia Sinica. His research interests include game theoretical models for communications networks, fog/edge computing design, and IoT systems. His passion for communication and networking was inspired by the work of Andrew Viterbi, who invented a communication decoding algorithm widely used in communications of space explorations nowadays, which broaden the horizon of human beings. The enthusiasm led him into researching the knowledge in communication and networking, also spurs him into pursuing his B.S. degree in electrical engineering and minor degree in economics at National Taiwan University.
